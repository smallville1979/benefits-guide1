HOW TO GET IT

Step by step. Spell it out. Dead simple.

1

Find out what you're owed — right now

Go to entitledto.co.uk — type in your situation. It tells you exactly which benefits you qualify for and how much. Takes 10 minutes. Completely free.

2

Claim Universal Credit

Go to gov.uk/universal-credit — make an account. Fill in your details. Done. If you're waiting for your first payment, ask immediately for an advance payment. They must give it you.

3

Get £150 off your energy bill

Go to your energy supplier's website. Search "Warm Home Discount." Click apply. If you're on UC, you probably get it automatically. If not, apply before the deadline. £150. Free.

4

Need food now? Here's how

Go to trusselltrust.org — find your nearest food bank. Call your GP or Citizens Advice and ask for a referral voucher. They give you 3 days of food. Free. No questions.

5

Emergency electric running out?

Call your energy supplier RIGHT NOW. Say: "I'm on a prepayment meter and I need emergency credit." They add credit instantly. You pay it back slowly later. Never go cold.

6

Free home upgrades — boiler, insulation

Search "ECO4 scheme" on Google. Go to your energy supplier's website and click ECO4. Fill in the form. If you qualify, they send someone out. New boiler, free insulation. You pay nothing.

7

Still stuck? One phone call sorts it all

Call Citizens Advice: 0800 144 8848 — free phone, free advice. Tell them your situation. They know every trick in the book and they're on your side. Free. Always.
